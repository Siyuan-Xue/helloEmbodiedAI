# Hardware and Sensors

Hardware and sensors include robots, tactile sensors, products, labs, and real-world platforms.

## Index Rules

- Use `type: product` for commercial or open hardware products.
- Use `type: lab` for labs and organizations.
- Use `type: code`, `dataset`, or `paper` when the resource is not primarily hardware.
- Add to Zotero only if there is a formal paper or citation need.

## Source Streams

- Awesome-Touch is the primary tactile hardware and lab stream.
- HCPLab-SYSU and zchoi provide robot/platform references.
