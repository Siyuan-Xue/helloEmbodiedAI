# Benchmarks

Benchmarks define measurable progress for embodied AI tasks.

## Index Rules

- Record task, metric, simulator/real-world setting, dataset dependency, paper, leaderboard, and source repository.
- Use `type: benchmark` in [[90_Sources/Resource Registry]].
- Add benchmark papers to Zotero and benchmark operation links to Obsidian.

## Common Benchmark Families

- Navigation: PointGoal, ObjectGoal, ImageGoal, VLN, EQA.
- Manipulation: grasping, rearrangement, dexterous manipulation.
- VLA: language-conditioned robot control and generalist policy evaluation.
- Sim-to-real: transfer, robustness, generalization, deployment.
