# Codebases

Codebases are indexed for reuse and reproduction. They do not need Zotero entries unless attached to formal papers.

## Index Rules

- Record project URL, paper URL, installation status, task, model family, dataset dependencies, and reproduction notes.
- Use `type: code` in [[90_Sources/Resource Registry]].
- Promote a codebase to a separate Obsidian note only when you plan to run, modify, or compare it.
