# Foundations

Foundations collect the mathematical and modeling tools that appear across embodied AI.

## Includes

- Deep learning, reinforcement learning, imitation learning, control, robotics.
- Diffusion, flow matching, generative modeling, sequence modeling.
- Vision encoders, language models, multimodal large models.

## Source Streams

- HCPLab-SYSU for survey-level foundations.
- MilkClouds for VLA prerequisites and reading order.
- zchoi for current frontier methods.
