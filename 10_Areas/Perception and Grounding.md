# Perception and Grounding

Perception turns sensor streams into representations that can support reasoning and action.

## Includes

- 2D/3D perception, object detection, segmentation, scene graphs.
- SLAM, mapping, localization, active perception.
- Visual grounding, language grounding, multimodal grounding.
- Tactile perception when it is used for state estimation or object understanding.

## Source Streams

- HCPLab-SYSU for embodied perception.
- ChanganVR for embodied vision and navigation perception.
- Awesome-Touch for tactile and visual-tactile sensing.
