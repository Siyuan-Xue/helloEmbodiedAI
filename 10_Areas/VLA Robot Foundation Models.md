# VLA / Robot Foundation Models

VLA models connect vision, language, and robot actions. This area is the bridge between multimodal foundation models and physical control.

## Includes

- RT-1, RT-2, OpenVLA, ACT, Diffusion Policy, pi0, GR00T, RL-VLA.
- Action tokenization, continuous action prediction, policy learning.
- Data scaling, efficient inference, adaptation, evaluation.

## Source Streams

- MilkClouds is the study roadmap.
- zchoi is the frontier update stream.
- Awesome-Touch contributes tactile VLA and contact-rich extensions.
