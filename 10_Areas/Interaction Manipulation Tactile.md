# Interaction, Manipulation, Tactile

Interaction is where embodied AI touches the world: grasping, manipulating, rearranging, and communicating.

## Includes

- Grasping, manipulation, rearrangement, dexterous control.
- Contact-rich tasks, tactile sensing, visual-tactile models.
- Human-robot interaction and language-conditioned operation.

## Source Streams

- HCPLab-SYSU for embodied interaction.
- Awesome-Touch for tactile resources, sensors, simulators, products, labs.
- zchoi and MilkClouds for VLA policies and robot foundation models.
