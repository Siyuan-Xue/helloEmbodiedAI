# Platforms, Robots, Simulators, Datasets

This area contains the physical and virtual substrate for embodied AI.

## Includes

- Robot embodiments: mobile robots, manipulators, humanoids, dexterous hands, legged robots.
- Simulators and interactive environments.
- Datasets, benchmarks, data collection pipelines, and evaluation suites.
- Sensors and hardware when they serve the embodied AI pipeline.

## Source Streams

- HCPLab-SYSU for simulators and datasets.
- zchoi for benchmarks, simulators, and robot foundation model infrastructure.
- Awesome-Touch for tactile sensors, products, labs, and simulation resources.
