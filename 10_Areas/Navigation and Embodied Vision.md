# Navigation and Embodied Vision

Navigation links perception, spatial memory, planning, and action in embodied environments.

## Includes

- PointGoal, ObjectGoal, ImageGoal, audio-visual navigation.
- Vision-language navigation, embodied question answering, exploration.
- Spatial reasoning, scene memory, map-based and map-free policies.

## Source Streams

- ChanganVR is the main source for embodied vision/navigation.
- HCPLab-SYSU provides taxonomy-level grounding.
- zchoi contributes frontier agent and benchmark links.
