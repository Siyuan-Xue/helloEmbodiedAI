# Vocabulary

Definitions below are short direct excerpts from Cambridge Dictionary entries. Chinese glosses are kept intentionally for vocabulary learning.

| Word | POS | Dictionary definition | CN gloss | Paper-context meaning | Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| nascent | adjective | "only recently formed or started, but likely to grow larger quickly" | 新生的；萌芽的 | Describes an emerging research area or capability. | Embodied AI is still a nascent research direction. |
| entail | verb | "to make something necessary, or to involve something" | 需要；涉及 | Used when one technical goal requires another condition or process. | Long-horizon tasks entail reliable memory and planning. |
| akin | adjective | "having some of the same qualities" | 类似的；相近的 | Used to compare two mechanisms or ideas. | A reactive world model can be akin to a learned reflex. |
| Kleenex | noun | "a brand name for a type of thin, soft paper" | 纸巾；面巾纸品牌 | Appears as an object in a spatial grounding or grasping instruction. | The keyboard is to the right of the Kleenex box. |
| granularity / granularities | noun | "the quality of including a lot of small details" | 粒度；细节程度 | Refers to how detailed subtasks or action units are. | Low-level action planning needs finer granularity. |
| scarcity | noun | "a situation in which something is not easy to find or get" | 稀缺；不足 | Describes the lack of high-quality embodied data. | Data scarcity slows embodied robot training. |
| intricate | adjective | "having a lot of small parts that are arranged in a complicated or delicate way" | 复杂精细的 | Describes complex manipulation or hand-task requirements. | Dexterous hands can perform intricate tasks. |
| prolong | verb | "to make something last a longer time" | 延长 | Used for extended duration, training time, or task execution. | Poor planning can prolong task execution. |
| quadruped | noun | "any animal that has four legs" | 四足动物；四足机器人语境中指四足形态 | Refers to four-legged robots in embodied robot categories. | Quadruped robots adapt well to uneven surfaces. |
| maneuverability | noun | "the quality of being easy to move and direct" | 机动性；操控灵活性 | Describes how easily a robot can move or be directed. | Tracked robots have strong off-road maneuverability. |
| dexterity | noun | "the ability to perform a difficult action quickly and skilfully with the hands" | 灵巧性；手部敏捷性 | Refers to fine manipulation ability in humanoid or robotic hands. | Humanoid robots need hand dexterity for fine-grained tasks. |
| imperative | adjective | "extremely important or urgent" | 极其重要的；迫切的 | Emphasizes a necessary research need. | It is imperative to study embodied AI for AGI. |
| pursuit | noun | "the act of trying to achieve a plan, activity, or situation" | 追求；实现某目标的努力 | Refers to pursuing AGI or a research goal. | Embodied AI matters in the pursuit of AGI. |
| realm | noun | "an area of interest or activity" | 领域；范围 | Refers to a conceptual area such as the physical realm or cyber realm. | Embodied agents bridge the cyber realm and the physical world. |
