# Research Roadmap

This roadmap turns the resource graph into a learning sequence. It favors conceptual leverage first, then specialization.

## Phase 0: Global Frame

- Read the core survey: [[30_Literature_Notes/Aligning Cyber Space with Physical World--A Comprehensive Survey on Embodied AI|Aligning Cyber Space with Physical World]].
- Build the mental split between [[10_Areas/Perception and Grounding|perception]], [[10_Areas/Interaction Manipulation Tactile|interaction]], [[10_Areas/Agents Planning and Memory|agents]], and [[10_Areas/World Models Sim2Real Evaluation|sim-to-real/world models]].
- Use HCPLab-SYSU as the first taxonomy source.

## Phase 1: Platforms and Inputs

- Understand robots, simulators, datasets, and benchmarks in [[10_Areas/Platforms Robots Simulators Datasets]].
- Track concrete assets in [[40_Resources/Datasets]], [[40_Resources/Simulators]], and [[40_Resources/Benchmarks]].
- Keep formal papers in Zotero and infrastructure links in Obsidian.

## Phase 2: Perception to Navigation

- Move from [[10_Areas/Perception and Grounding|perception and grounding]] to [[10_Areas/Navigation and Embodied Vision|navigation and embodied vision]].
- Use ChanganVR as the main resource stream for PointGoal, ObjectGoal, ImageGoal, VLN, EQA, and exploration.

## Phase 3: Interaction and Tactile Intelligence

- Study manipulation, rearrangement, dexterous control, and HRI in [[10_Areas/Interaction Manipulation Tactile]].
- Use Awesome-Touch to connect tactile sensors, visual-tactile models, data collection, simulators, products, and labs.

## Phase 4: Agents and VLA

- Connect high-level task planning to low-level action planning in [[10_Areas/Agents Planning and Memory]].
- Follow MilkClouds as the VLA study path and zchoi as the frontier tracker in [[10_Areas/VLA Robot Foundation Models]].
- Keep model families, datasets, and benchmarks connected through [[90_Sources/Resource Registry]].

## Phase 5: World Models, Sim-to-Real, Evaluation

- Use [[10_Areas/World Models Sim2Real Evaluation]] to integrate predictive models, interactive simulation, real2sim2real, robustness, and deployment.
- Track evaluation resources in [[40_Resources/Benchmarks]] and related Zotero collections.

## Reading Status Rules

- `inbox`: imported but not triaged.
- `to-read`: worth reading, not started.
- `reading`: actively reading.
- `read`: read and indexed.
- `core`: foundational or repeatedly referenced.
- `archived`: no longer relevant or superseded.
